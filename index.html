What the Fizz?

FIZZ Cooldrinks is a craft soda without any added bullshit, but plenty of real taste. Brought to you by the Cape Town Cool Drink Company, the makers of Juicebox, this fizzy fruit juice option delivers refreshing, authentic Apple, Red Grape and Pineapple flavours.

And it’s easy to see where that genuine quality comes from. For a start, only local fruits make it into our bottles – apples from the Elgin Valley, grapes from Stellenbosch and pineapples from Bathurst. These are then pressed right where they are picked, and bottled and capped in small batches by hand in Cape Town. Quality fruit juice mixed with carbonated water for a sparkle that fizzes you up!

FIZZ Cooldrinks contain neither preservatives not artificial sugars, and is made directly from the extracted juice. This means it’s not reconstituted from a concentrate to guarantee standardization of flavour. We prefer the original, real fruit taste – Local is Lekker!

All natural, no bullshit added just real fizz flavour...

Pineapple FIZZ

Tropical & Tasty

Splash into this bubble bath of tropical flavour and let your taste buds immerse in the creamy smooth bubbles of FIZZ. Packed with sun-drenched pineapples grown in the area, home of The Big Pineapple in South Africa. A semi-sweet, slightly tangy but full flavoured sparkling pineapple experience. Each sip brings you closer to the beach – try to pair me with some Coconutty stuff.

Grape FIZZ

Sweet & Smooth

This is something completely new, a non – alcoholic sensation. Our sparkling red grape juice is truly love at first sip. A combination of South Africa`s finest grapes, Cabernet Sauvignon and Shiraz, harvested in the Cape Winelands. We give it some secret way of pressing to impress your taste buds. A full flavoured, smooth, sweet toned refreshment with a kick of FIZZ.

Apple FIZZ

Crisp & Juicy

Less is more. Quench your thirst and let the bubbles tickle your lips. We press the juiciest, sun-ripened Golden Delicious and Granny Smith apples grown in the fertile Elgin Valley just outside of Cape Town and mix this up with refreshing fine bubbles of FIZZ. The result – a light, crisp and full flavoured soft drink, slightly sweet with a tangy bite. Get hooked on this delicious craft soda and enjoy the real FIZZ taste.
